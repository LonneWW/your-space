Your Space

This mockup application was created as the final project of the Master in Full Stack Development at start2Impact University.
Its purpose is to simulate a digital environment for writing and sharing notes between patient and therapist, in order to improve mutual coordination.
To test the application you need to use or create a demo account.
Please do not enter any personal or sensitive data, as this is a test environment.
The credentials provided at registration will only be used for demonstration and local testing purposes.
It is advisable to use a fictitious email address and without real references.
